My Journey
into Data analytics

Hi, my name is Chenille, and I am a data analyst with a background in public health and biology.

I am currently working as a Covid-19 Case Investigator in the state of Georgia. In this position, I have collected information through phone interviews and entered relevant data into the SendSS database for research purposes. While I have enjoyed this work, I have always been curious about the next steps in analyzing data. Within the scope of public health, this assessment of the data is called epidemiology, but I soon came across the much broader field of data analytics and was amazed at the possibilities.

On my journey to become a data analyst, I have learned SQL, Tableau and Python and have put those skills to use in the various projects included in this portfolio. I look forward to continuing to expand my skill set and seeing where the world of data takes me.
